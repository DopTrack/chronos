tooltip

found in: /source/css/project/components//_tooltip.scss

Tooltips are a quick way to provide extended information on a term or action on a page. The tooltips can be applied on words in the content and provides a subtle dotted line under the word to indicate the tooltip functionality. The tooltip fades in after a user hovers over the marked word. You can further specify whether you want the tooltip to appear (see example).
